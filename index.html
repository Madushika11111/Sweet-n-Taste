Who We Are

A Taste of Home, Every Day

Welcome to Sweet n Taste — your home for authentic, handmade Sri Lankan kavili. We bring the warmth of treasured family recipes to your door, not just at Avurudu, but every single day of the year.

Every sweet is crafted fresh to order using pure, natural ingredients — coconut, kithul treacle, rice flour and hand-selected spices — just like amma and achchi used to make.
